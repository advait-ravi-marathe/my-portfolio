The best movies I have seen so far

My top three movies of all-time.

October

This art film beautifully compares life of a young girl "Shivli" (played by Banita Sandhu) with the flower she has been named after. It shows a journey of unconditional love and support Shivli gets from his friend Dan (played by Varun Dhawan). It is one of the finest performances by Varun Dhawan.

Laila Majnu

This movie seems like a hopeless romantic film which potrays love story of Laila (played by Tripti Dimri) and Qais (played by Avinash Tewary). But things start taking turn in the second half when the movie starts exploring deeper, more passionate and crazy concepts of love. Again a superb performance by one of the underrated gems, Avinash Tewary.

Lootera

This film is set in the pre-independence era and shows love blossoming between Pakhi (played by Sonakshi Sinha) and Varun (played by Ranveer Singh) when Varun starts living as a tenant in Pakhi's house. However a major twist in their lives suddenly brews hate between the two. The film beautifully summarises the morale that one should be always optimistic and live life to the fullest.
